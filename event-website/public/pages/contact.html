FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers!

How far in advance should I book?

For weddings and large events, we recommend booking 6-12 months in advance. For smaller parties and celebrations, 2-3 months is usually sufficient. However, we can accommodate last-minute requests based on availability.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans. Typically we require a 25% deposit to secure your date, with the remaining balance divided into convenient installments leading up to your event.

Can you handle events of any size?

Absolutely! We've planned events from intimate gatherings of 20 guests to grand celebrations with over 2000 guests. Our team scales accordingly to ensure perfection at any size.

Do you plan destination events?

Yes! We specialize in destination weddings and events across India and internationally. We handle travel, accommodation, vendor coordination, and all logistics.

What's included in your packages?

Our packages include venue selection, decoration, catering, entertainment, photography, and coordination. Custom packages are also available based on your specific needs.

Can I meet the team before booking?

Of course! We offer free initial consultations at our office or via video call. We believe in building a personal connection with our clients.
