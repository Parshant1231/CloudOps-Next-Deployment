Who We Are

We Don't Just Plan Events, We Create Magic

Elegant Events was born from a simple idea: every celebration deserves to be perfect. Founded in 2010 by Priya and Rajesh Sharma, we started as a small team of passionate event planners with a vision to transform ordinary gatherings into extraordinary experiences.

Over the past 14 years, we've grown into one of India's most trusted event management companies, with a team of 50+ creative professionals who pour their heart into every event we touch. From intimate anniversaries to grand weddings, from corporate galas to celebrity parties – we've done it all with the same passion and attention to detail.

"Our philosophy is simple – we treat every event as if it's our own. Whether it's a wedding with 500 guests or a corporate meeting with 50 people, we bring the same level of dedication, creativity, and perfectionism."

— Priya Sharma, Founder

Our Mission

To transform our clients' dreams into reality by delivering exceptional event experiences that exceed expectations. We strive to create moments that become cherished memories, with unwavering commitment to quality, creativity, and personalized service. Every event we touch should reflect the unique story and vision of our clients, executed with flawless precision and artistic excellence.

Our Vision

To be India's most loved and trusted event management company, known for pushing creative boundaries and setting new standards in the events industry. We envision a future where Elegant Events becomes synonymous with perfection, innovation, and unforgettable experiences. We aim to expand our footprint across India while maintaining the personal touch and attention to detail that defines us.
